About me

There are a million things I want to be good at and coding is one of them. To bring out the value that is buried inside me, I sat in front of the computer a few months ago to learn to code and haven't got up since then.

After starting to build my own website, I was amazed how coding invoked my creative side and it quickly became one thing that keeps me up all night (without coffee).

The creative trigger in me is to be able to sleep soundly at night knowing that I have made at least one thing.
